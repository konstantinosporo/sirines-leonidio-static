SIRINES APARTMENTS - THE IDEAL ACCOMMODATION FOR TOTAL RELAXATION AND HOLIDAYS IN LEONIDION

Located on the beach of Leonidio, near the small harbor of Plaka, we have chosen to create 'SIRINES' Apartments for your accommodation.

The furnished apartments, 'SIRINES,' with an unobstructed view of the Myrtoan Sea, are the perfect choice for visitors of all ages.

Just 50 meters from our complex, you'll find the Plaka beach, inviting you to plunge into the waters of the Myrtoan Sea. Explore the traditional village of Leonidio through its narrow streets, beautiful squares, stately homes, and the charming Plaka harbor.

We invite you to relax, change your mood, escape the noise of the city, rejuvenate while watching the sun rise from the sea, and unwind as you listen to the gentle waves.

ATTRACTIONS & ACTIVITIES

Discover and explore Leonidio. Tap on the cards for entertainment suggestions!

1

LEONIDIO

Explore the narrow streets and picturesque squares of Leonidio. Discover the traditional architecture of the area through its stately homes and tower houses.

2

PARNON

Explore the mountains of the Parnon Range, which offer rich landscapes, unique views, and opportunities for activities such as hiking, mountain biking, and rock climbing.

3

MONASTERY OF PANAGIA ELONA

Visit the historic Monastery of Panagia Elonas, the imposing temple of Arcadia dating back to 1809 AD, built on a steep slope of Parnon.

4

THE SMALL HARBOR OF PLAKA

Relax by taking a stroll in the beautiful, picturesque harbor of Plaka and its taverns.

5

WATER SPORTS

Embark on a canoe or kayak to explore the coastal caves and shores of the Argolic Gulf, or indulge in a unique diving expedition of the seabed.

6

ROCK CLIMBING IN LEONIDIO

The Red Rock towering above Leonidio offers climbing routes suitable for all levels, providing an unforgettable experience.
